Warp Randomizer teleports you to a random room when you collect a pickup. Warps are always the same for each item within a single randomizer. For example, if you pick up the missile expansion behind your ship in Prime, reset, and pick it up again, it will teleport you to the same room until you generate a new randomizer. Skips the item loss at the beginning of the game. Removes the item pickup cutscenes and text boxes, and instead displays text informing you of what you picked up at the top of the screen. Randomizes the music played in each area that has its own, unique music.
